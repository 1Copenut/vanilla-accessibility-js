Thermal Boxing Club, 2016

I took this photo on a trip in October, 2016. We drove through Thermal, California on the way to the Salton Sea State Recreation Area. It might have been the long way around, but we arrived eventually. I liked the juxtaposition of the stucco buildings—built to last in the California heat—and the spray-painted message informing passers by this was the spot for local fighters wanting to train.
