Thermal Boxing Club. Thermal, CA. Circa 2016.

I took this photo on a trip in October, 2016. We drove through Thermal, California on the way to the Salton Sea State Recreation Area. It might have been the long way around, but we arrived eventually. I took this photograph because I liked the juxtaposition of the stucco buildings—buit to last in the California heat— and the spray-painted message informing passers by this was the spot local fighters used to train.
